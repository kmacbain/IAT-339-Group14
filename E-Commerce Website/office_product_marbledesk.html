Description

Inspired by the sculptural works of Salvador Dalí and Diego Giacometti, merges the rustic sensibility of hand-forged metal with cool Italian Carrara marble. Employing an ancient fabrication process, artisans hand hammer the metal bars to reveal an alive, imperfect and organic texture. The pounded, tapered silhouettes are a rich counterpoint to the smooth, honed stone.

65” W x 9” L x 60” H

  • Frame is forged of solid metal and hammered by hand

  • Forged Iron finish is crafted of solid iron
